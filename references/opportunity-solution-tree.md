# Opportunity Solution Tree (OST)

> Summary of Teresa Torres's framework from *Continuous Discovery Habits*.

## What it is
A visual framework that connects:
- **Outcome** (the business goal) → top of the tree
- **Opportunities** (unmet needs, pain points, desires) → middle layer
- **Solutions** (features, experiments) → bottom layer
- **Experiments** (tests to validate solutions)

## Why it matters for this tool
When assessing a stakeholder ticket, we are identifying **Opportunities** — not jumping to solutions. The OST reminds us to:
1. Map the ticket to a known outcome before acting
2. Ask: is this a symptom or a root cause?
3. Avoid solution-first thinking

## Key principles
- Separate opportunity discovery from solution ideation
- One outcome at a time — avoid competing goals
- Small, frequent discovery is better than big, infrequent research
- Stakeholder requests are signals, not requirements
